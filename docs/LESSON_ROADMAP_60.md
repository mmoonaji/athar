# Athar Beta Curriculum Roadmap (0 to 60 Lessons)

This roadmap outlines the plan to expand the Athar curriculum to 60 high-quality, verified lessons for the public beta release.

## Objective
To provide a foundational, engaging, and spiritually uplifting curriculum that spans the most essential Islamic sciences, designed specifically for daily 5-15 minute learning habits.

## Content Breakdown

### Domain 1: العقيدة (Aqeedah) - 20 Lessons
**Path: أركان الإيمان (Pillars of Faith)**
- Module 1: الإيمان بالله (Belief in Allah) - 5 Lessons
- Module 2: الإيمان بالملائكة والكتب (Angels & Books) - 5 Lessons
- Module 3: الإيمان بالرسل (The Messengers) - 5 Lessons
- Module 4: اليوم الآخر والقدر (The Last Day & Decree) - 5 Lessons

### Domain 2: الفقه (Fiqh) - 20 Lessons
**Path: فقه العبادات (Jurisprudence of Worship)**
- Module 1: الطهارة (Purification) - 5 Lessons
- Module 2: الصلاة: أركانها وواجباتها (Prayer: Pillars & Duties) - 7 Lessons
- Module 3: الزكاة والصيام (Zakat & Fasting) - 4 Lessons
- Module 4: الحج والعمرة (Hajj & Umrah) - 4 Lessons

### Domain 3: التزكية والأخلاق (Spiritual Purification & Ethics) - 10 Lessons
**Path: أعمال القلوب (Deeds of the Heart)**
- Module 1: الإخلاص واليقين (Sincerity & Certainty) - 5 Lessons
- Module 2: التوكل والرضا (Reliance & Contentment) - 5 Lessons

### Domain 4: السيرة النبوية (Prophetic Seerah) - 10 Lessons
**Path: مقتطفات من السيرة (Highlights from the Seerah)**
- Module 1: العهد المكي (Meccan Period) - 5 Lessons
- Module 2: العهد المدني (Medinan Period) - 5 Lessons

## Production Pipeline Strategy
1. **Specification Generation**: AI generates the `LessonSpec` for all 60 lessons simultaneously to ensure curriculum cohesion and prevent overlap.
2. **Review & Approval**: Editors review specs to ensure they match the `Content Bible` standards.
3. **Drafting**: AI generates the Markdown content based on the approved specs.
4. **Validation**: Reviewers manually verify Hadith authenticity, Quranic citations, and tone.
5. **Publishing**: Lessons are switched to `PUBLISHED` state, unlocking them for Beta Users.
